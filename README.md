**You don't choose between them yourself.** PikPak detects your country at checkout using your IP address, device language, and SIM card region. The right tier shows up automatically.

One important caveat: conversion from Regional to Global Premium is irreversible, and PikPak currently doesn't support refund requests for that switch. Know your situation before upgrading.
